KAIST

Visual Intelligence Lab

Welcome to the Visual Intelligence Lab (VILAB) at KAIST, led by Prof. Kuk-Jin Yoon. We focus on cutting-edge research in Computer Vision, Robotics, and Machine Learning. Our goal is to develop intelligent systems capable of understanding, reconstructing, and interacting with the complex real world.

Our core research areas include:

  • End-to-End Autonomous Driving
  • Generative AI & Multi-modal Language Models
  • Event-based Vision
  • 3D Scene Reconstruction & Understanding

We are looking for passionate students and researchers to join our journey.
If you're interested, please contact us at kjyoon@kaist.ac.kr
